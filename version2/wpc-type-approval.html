Product RF Site Survey Services

Survey your RF environment before you deploy — site analysis made precise.

Comprehensive RF site survey service for validation of wireless product deployment environments. Measures RF signal strength, interference sources, spectrum occupancy, channel utilisation, and coverage mapping across 370 MHz to 6 GHz. Suitable for Wi-Fi, LoRa, BLE, Zigbee, and cellular IoT products. Deliverable includes a detailed report with RSSI heatmaps, interference identification, and mitigation recommendations.
